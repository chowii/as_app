Help with your Sleep Tracker(s)

If you have purchased your Sleepsense with sleep tracking, your mattress should come with built in sensors - these are the USB cables exiting your mattress.

If you have a single mattress you should have one built-in sensor, with one USB cord hanging out of your mattress. If you have a double, queen, king, or split king, you should have two built-in sensors, with two USB cords hanging out.

  2. Ensure the USB cord(s) are plugged into the nearest power outlet
  3. On your phone or tablet, check that Bluetooth is turned on and Airplane mode is turned off
  4. Go back to the app
  5. Make sure your phone is near to your mattress and that there are no physical obstacles (including yourself) standing between your phone and the mattress
  6. Try connecting again
