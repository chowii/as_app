Help with your Adjustable Comfort System (Mattress Pump)

The Adjustable Comfort System (ACS) is the pump that controls the airflow to your mattress. For it to work it needs to be connected to three things:

  1. A power outlet
  2. The air hose(s) of your mattress
  3. The Bluetooth on your phone or tablet (if you’re using the Sleepsense app)

Having trouble connecting to the app?

Follow the steps below if you’re having trouble connecting to your mattress pump via the Sleepsense app.

  1. Check that it is plugged securely into a power outlet
  2. A blue light should flash for a few seconds to the left of where the power cord connects to the pump
  3. If the blue light does not turn on, there may be a fault with the pump. Please call support.

If you've followed the above steps and the blue light turns on but the app still won't connect to the pump, try checking the Bluetooth connection on your phone.

  1. Ensure Airplane Mode is not on
  2. Go to your phone's settings
  3. Go to 'Bluetooth'
  4. Ensure Bluetooth is turned on
  5. Go back to the app
  6. Make sure your phone is no further than 1 metre away from the pump and that there are no physical obstacles (including yourself) standing between your phone and the pump
  7. Try connecting again

If you've tried the above steps and are still having trouble, please contact support on 1300 654 000 and our customer care team will be happy to help.
